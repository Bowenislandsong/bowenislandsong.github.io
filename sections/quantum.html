3 Â· States & Measurement

Born rule: measuring $\lvert\psi\rangle = \alpha\lvert0\rangle+\beta\lvert1\rangle$ in the computational basis yields 0 with probability $\lvert\alpha\rvert^2$ and 1 with probability $\lvert\beta\rvert^2$. Postâ€‘measurement state collapses to the observed eigenstate.

Density matrices

For pure states $\rho = \lvert\psi\rangle\langle\psi\rvert$. Mixed states are convex combinations $\rho=\sum_k p_k \lvert\psi_k\rangle\langle\psi_k\rvert$.
