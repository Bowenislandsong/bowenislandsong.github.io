About Me

I am Bowen Song, a PhD student in Computer Science at University of Southern California, specializing in AI for Health and Explainable AI. My research focuses on developing interpretable machine learning models with low latency predictions for multi-modal sensor data, particularly for athlete injury prediction and sleep stage analysis.

I have practical industry experience from internships at eBay Inc. where I developed Mixture of Experts frameworks for ranking systems, and previous experience as a Software Engineer at Red Hat working on Kubernetes and OpenShift platforms. I am passionate about machine learning, cloud computing, and distributed systems.
